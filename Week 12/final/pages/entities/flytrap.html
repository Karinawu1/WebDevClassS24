Entity: Flytrap Humanoid

In the game, Inside the Backrooms, Flytrap Humanoid is found in the Grass Rooms (Level 7). The entity appears as a tall, blue, greenish creature made of vines, with the signiture flytrap as a head.


Information

A highly hostile mutant craeture, it is a humanoid comosed of roots and plants, it feeds on human flesh in large quantities, lacks vision but developed hearing and is thought to use the environment to its advantage.

It can adapt with the enviornment to hide and detect presences from a distance, as well as attack unexpectedly.

It is recommended to pay attention to the surroundings at all times, act stealthily and hide.


Entity Behavior

Not much is known about this entity at this time.


Strategies

  • The following photo was taken by a scout before he died, he got caught on something and couldn't escape.
  • Keep an eye out and avoid the entity as best as possible.
  • The entity is fast and is recommended best to hide and not get detected.
  • Use the fact that it can not see to your advantage.

Extra

If the player gets killed by this type of entity, the player will be awarded the ENTITY #172 #VICTIM achievement.
