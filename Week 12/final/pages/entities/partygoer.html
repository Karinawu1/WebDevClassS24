Entity: Partygoer

Partygoer is a tall, bipedal creature with yellow, smooth, leathery skin. They have no other functional features, with the exception of a cartoonish smile carved into their "face". It holds a balloon, adding to their appearance as if they are going to some kind of a party.


Information

A humanoid being lies in the backrooms, it likes to trick people with party objects but at the end, the entitiy will kill them and then it will collect their corpses, turning them into a bloody feast of human flesh and bones in order to convert them into cakes.

It is said that if you see a party balloon floating, the best option is to run away immediately.

The next photography was taken early moments before the expedition team were killed. It was recovered later by another expedition group on the office zone.


Entity Behavior

The Partygoer is an in-game entity that will wander around the present maze, or move through the walls of the candle maze. In the present maze, if the lights turn red, the Partygoer will look around for the player, if the player is in their line of sight, it will immediately chase them, whereas in the candle maze, it will simply chase the player, even walking through the red lines. In the present maze, the player has a chance to evade the Partygoer by running around a corner if they are far enough away. However, in the candle maze, the Partygoer will continuously pursue the player, making it impossible to escape once activated.


Strategies

  • The Partygoer appears when the player fails to collect all presents or candles within the designated time frame.
  • Finish the games before he can become active.
  • Mapping out the area can help.
  • You can neither hide or run from them.

Extra

If the player gets killed by this type of entity, the player will be awarded the ENTITY #67 #VICTIM achievement.
