Inside the Backrooms Guide

Levels Entities Items Achievements

BOOSTER/ASSISTING ITEMS

Item Name Use Spawn Location
Almond Water Heals your health to full and removes the blood stains around your screen. Found all over the place.
Anti-Anxiety Pills Reduces anxiety. (Reduces any screen blur/fuzz) Found all over the place.
Moth Jelly Gives nearly unlimited stamina for 2 minutes. A rare treat, spawns where it may be needed.
Flashlight Lights up the way ahead ('F' to activate) In the first drawer you bump into in the Dark Rooms.
Radio Used to communicate between players not in external communication. Found in the room before the elevator puzzle on top of the left drawer in the Lobby.

DARK ROOMS & PARKING ITEMS

Item Name Use Spawn Location
Clock Handle Needed for the clock puzzle. Randomized spawn.
Vhs Tape Needed for the VHS player atop the TV. Randomized Spawn.
Screw Driver Needed to close and ock vents to hinder the hound from getting back out of them. Found in the second drawer under the clock.
Hammer Needed to break the vases. In the locker in the room with the 9 dead bodies.
Red Key Needed to unlock the red door. In a vase.
Fuse Needed to restore the power in the Abandonded Office. In the letter-lock safe behind the Red Door.
Body Parts Needed for the 9 dead bodies puzzle. Only three are needed. See Vhs Tape. Randomized Spawn.
Plier Needed to break the lock of the final door in the Dark Rooms beyond the radioactive room. Found in the number lock safe.
Geiger Counter Needed for the radioactive room puzzle. Detects radioative level of objects. In the hazmat room beyond the switches puzzle.
Fire Extinguisher Needed for the radioactive room puzzle. Reduces the radioactive level of ojects. In the hazmat room beyond the switches puzzle.
Protection Suit Protects the wearer from radioactive hazards. In the hazmat room beyond the switches puzzle.
Valve Handle Needed to stop the leaking pipes in the Parking level. Scattered throughout the room.
Garage Access Card Lets you unlock the door in the Parking level. Found next to the dead body that the Skin Stealer was feeding on.

ABANDONDED OFFICE

Item Name Use Spawn Location
Motion Tracker Needed to detect Clumps in the Pool Rooms. Found in the rotary lock safe in the flipped room.
Abecedary Codex Translates the symbols on the monitors to input the code into the terminal. Found in the rotary lock safe in the flipped room.
Party Hat Lightens the mood... On the table of the 'fun' hazmat party guests.
Red Access Card Lets you unlock the final red door of the game, can also be used to open the door to the Sewerage, which is in the Parking. Found after completing the Partygoer's games on the table.

SEWERAGE ITEMS

Item Name Use Spawn Location
Water Treatment Key Used to open the door to the Waste Treatment Room. Found on the floor in the Sewerage next to a dead body. Will not always be in the same place. Hinted with a green glow.
Sewer Storage Room Key Used to open the door to the Storage Area. Found on the floor in the Sewerage nect to a dead body. Will not always be in the same place. Hinted with a green glow.
Metal Detector Used to identify locations of the medallions. Found on the floor in the waste room in a locked locker.
Rat Medallion Used to insert into the Medallion puzzle in the Waste Treatment Room. Found in the blocked-off section being held by a dead hazmat
Fish Medallion Used to insert into the Medallion puzzle in the Waste Treatment Room. Found in the sewage water in the blocked-off section. Requires the metal detector to pinpoint its locaton.
Parrot Medallion Used to insert into the Medallion puzzle in the Waste Treatment Room. Found in the sewage water in the blocked-off section. Requires the metal detector to pinpoint its locaton.
Oil Drum Fill it with oil to pour into the pipes of the dark Waste Treatement room to make the Clumps that were blocking your path hop onto the ceiling. Found on the floor in the Storage Room.
Bucket Can fill it with human meat to distract the Baby Rats in the Exit Room. Found in the Storage Room.
Gear Cog Used for the gear puzzle. Found where the statue used to be in the Grinder Room after completing the medallion puzzle.
Gear Handle Lever Used for gear puzzle. Found somewhere around the floor in the waste room, hinted with a green glow.
Sewer Exit Key Used to unlock the Sewerage Exit. Found in a cell in the counterweight room after the gear puzzle has been completed.
Bucket Can fill it with human meat to distract the Baby Rats in the Exit Room. Found in the Storage Room.
