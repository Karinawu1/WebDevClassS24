Entity: Death Moth

In the game, Inside the Backrooms, Death Moth is found in the Terror Hotel (Level 6). Death Moths look like Moths from Real Life but there have a Skull on there Back.


Information

In the old and abandonded corridors are giant flying insects, distinguished by a figure that resembles a skull on their back.

They are attacted to light and more when the source hits them directly, however, when they do not have visbility, it is difficult for them to find threats.

They are extremely territorial and aggressive, so if you see one run and don't look back.


Entity Behavior

To survive/avoid a Death Moth in the Hallway Section, do not shine the Flashlight at them. JUST DONT DO IT.


Strategies

  • Pretty fast
  • One Hit on Easy, Normal and Hard
  • Kinda easy to dodge in the Hallways, Impossible to dodge in the Moth's Nest
  • Extremly complex AI

Extra

If the player gets killed by this type of entity, the player will be awarded the ENTITY #4 #VICTIM achievement.
