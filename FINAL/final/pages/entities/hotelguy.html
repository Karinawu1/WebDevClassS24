Entity: Receptionist

In the game, Inside the Backrooms, the Receptionist is found in the Terror Hotel (Level 6). The entity appears as a humanoid, wearing a gas mask. They become hostile when they see non-hotel guests.


Information

Almost nothing is known about these entities, they are humanoids that wear a non-human face.

Apparently, they watch the hotel as they wander through all the rooms and corridors, they have been seen vomitting a green liquid which is highly harmful.

Be careful not to run into any of them, because they are quite hostile to those who are not hotel guests.


Entity Behavior

It is rumored that they like to eat the cocoons of the moths that inhabit the area. Very rarely they have been seen eating in the main dining room.


Strategies

  • Avoid being seen by these entities if all possible.
  • Keep an eye out and avoid the green liquid they throw up in the halls.
  • The entity is fast and is recommended best to hide.
  • Hide in the closet if you suspect you've been found.

Extra

If the player gets killed by this type of entity, the player will be awarded the ENTITY #18 #VICTIM achievement.
